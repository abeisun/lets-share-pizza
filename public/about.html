I Want Pizza, but I Don't Want a Whole One

Have you ever wanted a nice slice of pizza, but you have then run into the problem of being forced to buy an entire pie? Well, have no fear! I Want Pizza, but I Don't Want a Whole One is here to save your pizza deprived self!

How to Use:

  1. Browse the map for people with similar pizza interests near you
  2. Click on them on the map, enter your info, contact that person
  3. Enjoy your pizza and be happy you didn't have to buy an entire pie!

If that didn't work then...

  1. Create a new pizza pie request and enter your information
  2. Wait for some pizza lovers to add to your place on the map
  3. Contact people if they didn't contact you and order your pizza
  4. Enjoy your pizza and be happy you didn't have to buy an entire pie!
